<p>Building a website is a lot more complicated than just HTML. There's also the choice of server-side technologies, hosting platform and provider, and figuring out how to be found on the web. Here are some tools that can help.</p>
<p>
<dl>
<dt><a href="https://siteexplorer.search.yahoo.com/">Yahoo Site Explorer</a></dt>
<dd>Yahoo's webmaster tools</dd>

<dt><a href="http://webmaster.live.com/">Microsoft's Webmaster Tools</a></dt>
<dd>Sitemap and MSNbot crawling stats</dd>


<dt><a href="https://www.google.com/webmasters/tools/">Google's Webmaster Tools</a></dt>
<dd>Sitemap, site info, Googlebot crawling stats</dd>

<dt><a href="http://www.alexa.com/siteowners">Alexa site </a></dt>
<dd>statistics and rankings</dd>


<dt><a href="http://feedburner.google.com">Feedburner</a></dt>
